Northwestern Graduate Programs in Healthcare Quality & Patient Safety

 

There is a great need for ALL healthcare professionals to develop expertise and leadership in the fields of healthcare quality and patient safety.

 

Northwestern faculty and full-time employees are eligible for a tuition discount of 90 percent through the Employee Reduced Tuition Benefits Program to pursue graduate education at Northwestern.

 

Northwestern offers three graduate programs in Healthcare Quality & Patient Safety designed for high performing healthcare professionals including faculty, staff, fellows, residents and medical students.

 

PROGRAMS

•Two-Year Master of Science Degree Program
•One-Year Certificate Program
•Full-Time PhD Program

 

PROGRAM HIGHLIGHTS

•Executive-style, interdisciplinary education model designed to accommodate full-time working healthcare professionals
•NEW CURRICULUM FORMAT consists of four 5-day Immersion Sessions accompanied by Distance-Learning Sessions held remotely
•Programs begin every summer quarter
•Study with the highest caliber of peers

 

FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE

•Northwestern University Employee Reduced Tuition Benefit
•Northwestern University Reduced Tuition for Residents and Fellows
•Scholarship opportunities available for nurses, allied health professionals and clinicians caring for underserved populations
