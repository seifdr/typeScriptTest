Hardware Purchasing

The devices below comprise the standard hardware available through Feinberg IT. These items have been tested and selected to meet our environment and security standards. All devices using Feinberg funds must be purchased through Feinberg IT. 

Visit our Frequently Asked Questions page to help find answers to our most commonly asked questions about the ordering process. You can also contact Feinberg IT directly at fsmhelp@northwestern.edu.

Shopping Cart

To submit your IT Hardware Request Order Form, simply add items to your shopping cart by clicking the "Add to Cart" button below. Once you are finished shopping, you will be prompted to complete the form. You will not be charged or asked to provide any payment information.
